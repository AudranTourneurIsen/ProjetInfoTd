Once upon a time during the Non-Euclidian era, the Polygon Kingdoms were invaded by the Circle Monarchies. A great Hero, Enneatriacontagon, rose up to rally the kingdoms together and push back the Circles United Monarchies to their territory. After countless fierce battles, Enneatriacontagon led the newly founded Federation of Allied Polygon to victory.

After multiple centuries of peace, the Circles Monarchies managed to build back their power and form a new army, more powerful than ever.

It is now your role to become the new hero, and protect the Federation of Allied Polygon from the invaders !
